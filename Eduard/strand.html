Our Strands!

Strand!

The academic strand aims to equip students with the knowledge, skills, and critical thinking abilities
needed to succeed in higher education and pursue their chosen career paths effectively.

Technical-Vocational and Livelihood (TVL)

Information and Communications Technology (ICT).
Home Economics and Livelihood Education (HELE).

Humanities and Social Sciences (HUMSS)

covers topics in the liberal arts, training students to think, write, and speak about various humanistic and societal concerns.

Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics (STEM)

Through the STEM strand, senior high school students are exposed to complex mathematical and science theories and
concepts which will serve as a foundation for their college courses.

Accountancy, Business and Management (ABM)

It focuses on teaching students the basic concepts of financial management, project management, marketing, human resources, modern management trends, organizational behavior, and more.
